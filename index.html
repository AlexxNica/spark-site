What is Spark?

Spark is a platform for launching your SaaS business. It contains all the code you need to do subscription billing, authentication, invoice PDFs, profile photos, teams, and more. Basically, developers install Spark and all of the screens and logic to handle all of the user registration, subscriptions, invoices, authentication etc is done for you so you can just get started building whatever idea you have.

Authentication

Authentication, registration, and password reset are ready out of the box. It’s never been faster to get started building your dreams.

Subscription

Subscription billing without the hassle. Supports monthly and yearly plans powered by Stripe or Braintree. Define your plans using fluent, expressive code, and get started building what matters: your business.

Invoices

Allow your customers to download PDF copies of their invoices. Spark will even automatically e-mail your customers their monthly invoice. One more thing you don’t have to worry about.

Customizable

Need to customize a piece of Spark? No problem. Replace small pieces of entire chunks of Spark with just a few lines of code. You can even extend Spark’s JavaScript. Tailor Spark to your needs or stick with the defaults - it’s up to you!

Teams

Allow your users to create, join, and invite other users to their teams without writing a single line of code. Weeks of writing boilerplate is so last year.

Extendable Backend

Spark ships with Kiosk, an extendable developer back-end. View your yearly and monthly revenue, make application announcements, and more. Take Kiosk to the next level by adding your own modules.

User Impersonation

Debugging a problem and need to login as one of your customers? Spark’s developer Kiosk allows you to authenticate as any of your customers.

Two-Factor Authentication

Want to offer two-factor authentication to your customers. Spark can handle that for you with a couple lines of configuration. It’s all powered by Authy, an industry leader in secure two-factor authentication.
